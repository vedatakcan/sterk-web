Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sterk mobile application ("App") collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The App is designed with a data minimization approach:

  • Personal Data: The App does not directly collect, store, or share personal information such as name, email, phone number, or location.
  • Local Data: Game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on the user’s device only.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party advertising services in order to remain free. In this context, Google AdMob is used.

Third-party advertising providers may use device identifiers (such as IDFA), cookies, and similar anonymous identifiers to serve and personalize ads. These processes are not directly controlled by the App developer.

Users can manage ad personalization preferences through their device settings and control tracking permissions via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.

For more information, please review Google Privacy Policy.

3. Data Security

The App implements reasonable technical measures to protect user data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
