Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sterk mobile application ("App") collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The App is designed with a data minimization approach:

The App uses Google AdMob to remain free. Third-party providers may use device identifiers (IDFA/AAID) to serve personalized ads.

For more details, see Google Privacy Policy.

We implement technical measures to protect your local data. However, no digital storage is 100% secure.
